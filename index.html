Overview

Mission & Background

The clinical need, mission, and engineering approach behind PureMed Zenith.

Mission Statement

→ Develop the next generation of GLP-1 therapy autoinjectors for all patients, that are reusable, tunable and safe, to optimize patient use and outcomes.

Clinical Background

→ Self-administered subcutaneous injections are frequently associated with high rates of patient non-adherence due to needle phobia and pain caused by uncontrolled, high speed injection delivery. Conventional autoinjectors rely on a strict mechanical design that utilizes a spring, delivering the medication at a fixed rate. This process cannot adapt to the patients tissue resistance leading to non-uniform delivery, inconsistent dosing, and local damage.

Problem Statement

→ Over 37 million Americans require regular subcutaneous therapy for metabolic disorders, yet the market is dominated by a "one-size-fits-all" delivery system that fails to address specific physiological differences amongst patients. Existing solutions also create significant medical waste and lacks a variety of feedback mechanisms that would ensure a more consistent, pain minimized injection experience for all patients.

Our Approach

→ At PureMed we designed an autoinjector known as the Zenith. This electromechanical autoinjector operates as closed loop feedback control system that monitors real-time pressure feedback readings to modulate a motor driven adaptive flow profile during the injection. By integrating precise mechatronics, this device maintains optimal delivery speeds regardless of the tissue resistance, with validated performance data collected through mechanical testing and analysis.

Adaptive Flow Profile (AFP) Algorithm

The AFP algorithm reads real-time pressure sensor data and modulates actuator speed across four defined delivery zones, adjusting delivery speed based on severity of resistance and stopping automatically when a safety threshold is exceeded.

Zone Speed Description
Zone 1 Fast Minimal tissue resistance detected: full-speed advance
Zone 2 Medium Moderate resistance present: controlled advance
Zone 3 Slow Elevated resistance: cautious step advance
Safety Stop Stop Force threshold exceeded: delivery halted immediately
